# PGP
PGP(Pretty Good Privacy) is a tech for protect the safety of data.
In internent, our email, files in drives, messages and so on are often saved in servers as plain data so it is important to protect your privacy and data safety. PGP is a tool for encryption, decryption and digital signature for data in internet. It is used for protection safety of email and file data.

Everyone has a key pair contains a public key and a private key. Public key for encryption verify the signature and privte key for decryption and signing. It is high security and needless to exchange the keys before it but complex to use.
